ABOUT US

Our relentless pursuit of innovation and excellence drives us to provide unparalleled automation and power solutions, empowering businesses to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.

Our mission is to provide our customers with superior products that are reliable, robust, efficient, and of the highest quality. By doing so, we empower our customers to achieve peak performance and success in their respective industries.

Designed for future,
Dare to innovate.

Origin Tech Private Limited is a leading manufacturer of electric and electronic products that cater to various industries’ automation and power solution needs. The company prides itself on its experienced R&D team, led by Dr. Anand Kakade, who holds a Ph.D. from IIT Kharagpur and has over 14 years of experience in product design.
As a first-of-its-kind organization with over eight years of experience in manufacturing, Origin Tech has gained tremendous expertise in the automation and power industry. The company offers a range of high-quality products, including voltage stabilizers, industrial UPS, smart output driver cards, and IoT automation solutions for various industries.
Origin Tech’s commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has made it one of the most trusted names in the industry. The company’s R&D team is constantly engaged in developing new products and improving the existing ones to meet the ever-changing needs of its customers.
One of Origin Tech’s core strengths is its in-house manufacturing facilities, which are equipped with the latest machinery and technology to ensure that its products are of the highest quality. The company’s state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities are designed to meet global quality standards, and its products undergo rigorous testing to ensure that they are reliable and meet the needs of its customers.
